# Seed Channels

Initial list of YouTube channels to crawl for background music mixes. Channels will be seeded one at a time, with manual review of each mix before it enters the catalog.

## Seeding Strategy

- Crawl **one channel at a time**
- Manual review of each mix before it enters the searchable catalog
- Quality over quantity — only high-quality mixes with good audio and smooth transitions
- Target: ~1,000-2,000 curated mixes over 2-3 weeks
- Keyword search discovery will fill genre gaps after initial seed
